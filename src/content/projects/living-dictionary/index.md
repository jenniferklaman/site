Collaborated with UF's MELD Lab and the Mijikenda community to build a **standardized picture dictionary** using the Online Living Dictionary platform.  

- Curated and annotated multimodal entries, including images, audio recordings, and orthographic data.  
- Designed data entry workflows to ensure **consistent linguistic and cultural representation**.  
- Managed and organized contributions to maintain **accuracy and accessibility** for both researchers and the Mijikenda community.  
- Integrated structured metadata to support **searchable, multilingual dictionary content**.
